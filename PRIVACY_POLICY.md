# Privacy Policy for Compound Interest Calculator App

**Effective Date: November 29, 2025**

## Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how the Compound Interest Calculator App ("App"), developed by Ranjit (GitHub: ranjit53), collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Information We Collect
- **No Personal Data**: The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, such as names, emails, device IDs, or location data.
- **Usage**: All calculations (e.g., compound interest with Nepali B.S. calendar) are performed locally on your device. Inputs are not saved or shared.

## How We Use Information
- No data is used for advertising, analytics, or third-party sharing.

## Sharing with Third Parties
- None. The App has no integrations with external services.

## Children's Privacy
- The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their data.

## Security
- Local processing ensures data stays on your device.

## Changes to This Policy
